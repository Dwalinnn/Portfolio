About me

Junior Technical Artist | Video Game Development

Currently pursuing my Master 2 in Video Game Art at HEAJ, I specialize in technical artistry for video games, leveraging tools such as Unity and Unreal Engine. My expertise includes a wide range of industry-standard software such as Unreal Engine, Unity, Blender, Maya, Substance Painter, Substance Designer, Houdini, JangaFX softwares and Reaper.
With a passion for the digital creative industry, I’m a fast learner, autonomous, and love finding innovative, creative solutions to complex challenges. My commitment to continuous improvement makes me confident in my productivity, and I always remain proactive and fully invested in any project I undertake.
